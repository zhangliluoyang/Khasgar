Travel to Khasgar

Kashgar, officially known as Kashi, is an oasis city in Xinjiang, China. It is one of the westernmost cities of China, near the border with Afghanistan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, and Tajikistan.

With a population of over 500,000,Kashgar has served as a trading post and strategically important city on the Silk Road between China, the Middle East, and Europe for over 2,000 years.

It's a place of cultual convergence, exotic seneries and wonderful food.
